### **3. Weighting Principles**

What principles should guide the weights?

**Proposed Principles:**
- **Multiply minimum:** Always at least 5-10% (even in crisis, build for future)
- **Essentials minimum:** Always at least 20-25% (cover basics)
- **Enjoyment maximum:** Cap at 45% (prevent overspending)
- **Total:** Always exactly 100%
- **Differentiation:** Each priority should feel distinct (no duplicates)
